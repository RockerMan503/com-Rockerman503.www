Moonshine Tavern, located in Hong Kong, is a modern on taverns of old. Come in on Tavern Tuesdays for half off drinks and specials

Our Goal: Our goal here at Moonshine Tavern is to create a feeling of a medieval tavern with the modern luxuries of today. When it comes to dim sum, mixed drinks, and appetizers, our food speaks for itself. Our favorite review of our fair tavern is by Chua Lam’s review from March, 2016:

“This tavern may seem like a run-of-the-mill modern restaurant, but their food and atmosphere make me feel like I’m at home.” -Chua Lam, March 2016.

We serve only the finest alcohol brands brought from the best parts of China, so you don't have to worry about your liquor choice.
